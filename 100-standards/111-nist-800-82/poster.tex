\section{\textcolor{accent}{\faIcon{file-alt}}\hspace{0.4em}Overview}

NIST SP 800-82 is the primary U.S. government guidance for securing OT systems including ICS, SCADA, DCS, PLCs, RTUs, and SIS. Rev. 3 (2023) expanded scope from ICS to all OT and aligned with NIST CSF and SP 800-53.

\posterinfo{
\textbf{Authoritative} (primary US federal OT guidance), \textbf{comprehensive} (threats, vulnerabilities, countermeasures), \textbf{practical} (implementation guidance with overlay controls), and \textbf{referenced} by regulators worldwide.
}

\section{\textcolor{accent}{\faIcon{shield-alt}}\hspace{0.4em}Security Controls (OT Overlay)}

CM & Config Mgmt & Change control, baselines \\
CP & Contingency & Backup, recovery, continuity \\
IA & Ident/Auth & Account mgmt, strong auth \\
IR & Incident Resp & OT-specific procedures \\
MA & Maintenance & Secure remote, vendor access \\
SC & Sys/Comm Prot & Segmentation, encryption \\
\end{tabular}

\posterdanger{
\textbf{High-priority controls:} Network segmentation, secure remote access with MFA, application whitelisting, comprehensive logging/monitoring, regular vulnerability assessments, IR planning and testing.
}

\section{\textcolor{accent}{\faIcon{tasks}}\hspace{0.4em}Implementation}

\postersuccess{
\textbf{Phased approach:}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textcolor{success}{\faIcon{check}}\hspace{0.2em}\textbf{Assessment:} Inventory assets, assess current state
    \item \textcolor{success}{\faIcon{check}}\hspace{0.2em}\textbf{Planning:} Develop security plan, prioritize
    \item \textcolor{success}{\faIcon{check}}\hspace{0.2em}\textbf{Implementation:} Deploy controls incrementally
    \item \textcolor{success}{\faIcon{check}}\hspace{0.2em}\textbf{Operations:} Monitor, maintain, improve
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{Aligns with:} NIST CSF, ISA/IEC 62443, NERC CIP, ISO 27001/27002.
}

\postertip{
NIST 800-82 recommends the \textbf{Purdue Model} or IEC 62443 zone concepts for network architecture. Use the OT overlay to tailor SP 800-53 controls -- start with gap analysis and prioritize based on risk.
}
